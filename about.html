A Tradition of Growth

Founded in 1924, Makindye Secondary School began as a visionary endeavor to provide holistic education in an environment that honors both character and intellect.

Over the past century, we have evolved from a small local boarding house into a premiere international preparatory institution. Our history is etched in the success stories of our alumni—leaders in science, the arts, and public service. We have consistently expanded our campus while preserving the heritage of our original stone library, ensuring every student feels the weight of the legacy they are now a part of.
